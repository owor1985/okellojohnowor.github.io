📌 PROJECT STATUS UPDATE @PP NEWTON HIGH SCHOOL GULU UNYAMA

Dear Parents, Stakeholders, and Well-wishers,

We are pleased to share the progress on key development projects aimed at improving the learning environment at PP Newton High School Gulu Unyama. Below is a summary of completed and ongoing initiatives.

✅ COMPLETED PROJECTS

  

 

   🚧 UNFINISHED PROJECTS  

Science Laboratory

A fully equipped science laboratory is now operational. Students can now engage in practical learning in Biology, Chemistry, and Physics, enhancing performance in sciences and preparing them for national examinations.

    

Football Pitch

Work on the standard football pitch is still underway. Drainage leveling and grass planting are in progress. Additional funding is required to install goalposts and perimeter markings.

Girls’ Dormitory

A safe and spacious dormitory has been fully completed and is now housing our female students. The facility includes adequate ventilation, secure bedding areas, and improved sanitation to promote dignity and focus on academics.

    

Additional Classrooms (if applicable – adjust as needed)

Construction of two more classrooms is ongoing to decongest current spaces. The roofing and plastering are pending completion.

 

🔜 Next Steps

We call upon parents, alumni, NGOs, and government partners to support the completion of the remaining projects. Every contribution brings us closer to a holistic learning environment.
