P.P NEWTON HIGH SCHOOL

PP is an abbreviation for Pons Paul. Newton is a great Scientist and Economist which this School adopted. Pons Paul Okot was the father of Hon Okot John Amos the proprietor of PP Newton High School. Pons Paul was one of the many Acholi who loved education so much that this school is given his name to honor him.

H/M. REV. CANON OBONYO PATRICK JAMES

The school was started in the year 2019 and is located North East of Gulu City, 8 kilometers along Gulu -Kitgum road. It is both Ordinary and A level mixed and serves as Day and Boarding. It started with the aim of providing quality and affordable education in Northern Uganda.
